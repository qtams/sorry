My dearest Adi,

I never wanted to make your heart heavy. You deserve love that feels safe, calm, honest, and sure. You deserve someone who listens better, understands deeper, and chooses you even on difficult days. I want to be that person for you.

Please do not give up on us. I know not every day is easy, but I still believe in what we have. I believe in our love, in our bond, and in the beautiful future we can still build together.

I promise to be more patient with you, more careful with my words, and more present in the moments that matter. I will show my love through the way I treat you, the way I protect your feelings, and the way I keep choosing us.

I want more days with you, more laughs with you, more late-night talks, more little memories, and more dreams that we will turn into real moments together. I want to keep choosing you, loving you, and growing with you.

When I think about the future, I hope it is a future that still has us side by side. I hope we keep holding on to each other, keep understanding each other, and keep fighting for the kind of love that lasts.

Please trust that my love is real. I may not always be perfect, but my heart is sincere. I am willing to learn, to change, to improve, and to become better for you and for us.

You are someone I never want to lose. If you let me, I will keep proving that my love is real, steady, and full of hope. I want us to have more beautiful tomorrows together.

Thank you for loving me, for being part of my life, and for giving my heart a reason to believe again. No matter what happens, please remember that what I feel for you is sincere and deeply true.

I am here, I am trying, and I am not giving up on you. I hope one day, when you look at me, you will feel the assurance that you are loved, valued, respected, and chosen every single day.

With all my love,

yours, with all my love and hope for our future
